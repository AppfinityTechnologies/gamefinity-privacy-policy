Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Gamefinity Android application, which is currently in development and planned for future release on the Google Play Store. This policy will govern the app's data practices once it becomes publicly available, and by using Gamefinity after launch, you agree to the practices described in this document.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains exactly what information will be collected when you use our app, why it will be collected, and how it will be used, once the app is live. We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

If you do not agree with this policy, please refrain from using the application when it becomes available. Use of Gamefinity after its public release will constitute your acceptance of these terms.

🗂️

Information We Collect

We and our third-party SDK partners may collect the following information automatically during normal use of the app:

InformationPurpose
Device Advertising ID Serve and measure personalised advertisements via Google AdMob
App usage events & sessions Understand gameplay behaviour and improve features via Firebase Analytics
Crash logs & stack traces Detect and fix app crashes using Firebase Crashlytics
In-app purchase tokens Validate and fulfil purchases via Google Play Billing
IP address & approximate country Serve region-appropriate ads and comply with local laws
Device model, OS version, language Ensure app compatibility and optimise performance

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other directly identifying personal information unless you voluntarily provide it when contacting us.

⚙️

How We Use Information

Information collected is used only for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the Gamefinity application
  • To display relevant advertisements and generate revenue that supports free gameplay
  • To analyse in-app behaviour and guide improvements to features and difficulty
  • To detect, investigate, and resolve crashes and technical errors
  • To process and verify in-app purchase transactions
  • To comply with applicable laws, Google Play policies, and AdMob policies

We do not use your data for automated profiling that produces significant legal or similarly significant effects on you.

🔐

Permissions Used

Gamefinity requests only the Android permissions strictly necessary for its operation:

PermissionPurpose
INTERNET Load advertisements, send analytics events, and process in-app purchases
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Check connectivity before making network calls to avoid unnecessary errors
BILLING Required by Google Play to initiate and complete in-app purchase transactions
AD_ID (Android 13+) Access the Google Advertising ID for personalised ad serving and measurement
VIBRATE Provide optional haptic feedback during in-game interactions
WAKE_LOCK Keep the screen active during an active gameplay session
📢

Google AdMob Advertising

Gamefinity uses Google AdMob, an advertising service by Google LLC, to display banner, interstitial, and rewarded advertisements within the app.

Collects Advertising ID, device information, IP address, ad interaction data, and approximate location.
Purpose Deliver relevant ads to support free gameplay without charging users upfront.
Policy policies.google.com/privacy

You may opt out of personalised ads at any time via Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation on your Android device. Non-personalised ads will still appear.

📊

Firebase Analytics Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how players engage with Gamefinity and to make data-driven decisions that improve the experience.

Collects App events, session duration, screen views, in-app actions, device model, OS version, and country (derived from IP).
Purpose Measure user engagement, track feature usage, and guide future app updates.
Retention Analytics data is retained for up to 14 months per Google's default policy.
Policy firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Analytics data is aggregated and does not personally identify you. No names, emails, or contact details are linked to analytics events.

🛠️

Firebase Crashlytics Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically detect, record, and diagnose app crashes in real time, allowing us to release fixes quickly.

Collects Stack traces, crash timestamps, device model, OS version, app version, and a randomly generated installation UUID.
Purpose Identify the root cause of crashes and stability issues to improve app reliability.
No PII Crashlytics does not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifying information.
Policy firebase.google.com/support/privacy
💳

Google Play Billing Purchases

Gamefinity offers optional in-app purchases — such as premium features, ad removal, or virtual currency — processed entirely through Google Play Billing.

Collects Purchase token, product ID, purchase timestamp, and transaction status. Payment card details are handled exclusively by Google.
Purpose Validate and fulfil in-app purchase transactions securely.
Policy play.google.com/about/play-terms/

We never have access to your payment card details. For refunds or billing disputes, contact Google Play Support directly through your Google account.

We are not responsible for the data practices of these third parties. Data shared with these services is governed by their respective terms and privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to safeguard your information, including:

  • All data transmitted between the app and third-party services is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • We do not operate our own servers that store personal user data
  • Access to analytics dashboards and crash reports is restricted to authorised developers only
  • Third-party SDKs are kept up to date to address known security vulnerabilities

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

👶

Children's Privacy

Gamefinity is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately using the details below. We will take prompt steps to delete such information from our records.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the minimum age of consent is 16, unless local law specifies a lower age (minimum 13). Users below this age should use the app only under parental supervision.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Deletion — Request erasure of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Opt-out of personalised ads — Via your device's advertising settings
  • Data portability — Request your data in a portable format where applicable
  • Withdraw consent — Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address listed below. We will respond within 30 days. Note that some data is collected directly by Google's SDKs; for that data, you may need to exercise your rights through Google's own privacy controls.
